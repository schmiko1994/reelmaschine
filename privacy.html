Privacy Policy – Reelmaschine

Last updated: 2025

Reelmaschine (“we”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our platform.

Data We Collect

We only collect the minimum required data to operate the service. This includes your email address, connected social media accounts and encrypted OAuth tokens.

OAuth & Account Permissions

We do not collect or store passwords. All connections to TikTok, YouTube, Instagram and Snapchat are handled via OAuth. Tokens are encrypted using AES-256 and can be revoked at any time.

Generated & Uploaded Content

Videos created or uploaded through the service are temporarily stored to complete the publishing workflow and deleted automatically after processing.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. Data is only transmitted to the social platforms you connect for the purpose of publishing your content.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account or connected tokens at any time by contacting support@reelmaschine.com.
